Spotlight gifting

A delightful and customizable product to fulfil various corporate gifting use cases.

Zeta Spotlight was conceptualized as a comprehensive gifting platform catering to various corporate gifting usecases - from festive gifting to milestone awards, special occasions to incentive programmes, employee gift cards to brand vouchers.

This platform, in addition to Zetaâ€™s core Optima product (a tax-benefits platform) would help us offer corporates a complete suite to cater to their employee engagament needs.

Designing Spotlight gifting

On a product level, two main choices were available to the corporates:

  • Send customized digital gift cards (either by signing up with Zeta or integrating their existing rewards and recognition platform with Zeta).

  • Hand out physical gift cards in beautiful (but not customizable) packaging.

The first task while designing the Spotlight platform was to clearly pitch these product offerings to the corporates.

Since Zeta had significant number of corporates already using the Optima tax benefits product, a compelling product pitch for Spotlight gifting within the Optima dashboard could do wonders to generate organic signups for the new product.

Pitching the product

As we were building this product based on requests from corporates, we had a good idea about the scenarios in which each product would be used. After talking with a few more corporates to validate this, we decided the messaging to be as follows:

  • Digital gift cards are customizable and easy to distribute.

  • Physical gift cards are simple to use for anyone familiar with debit/credit cards i.e. everybody.
